# Integrations


> Learn about the available integrations with Clerk.

## Connect with databases and more


  - [Vercel Marketplace](/guides/development/integrations/platforms/vercel-marketplace)
  - Configure and integrate Clerk into your Vercel projects through the Vercel Marketplace.
  - 

  ---

  - [Convex](/guides/development/integrations/databases/convex)
  - Clerk offers a first-class integration with Convex, the serverless state management platform.
  - 

  ---

  - [Firebase](/guides/development/integrations/databases/firebase)
  - Clerk integrates directly with Firebase, a popular Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) provider, so developers can easily add our beautiful Sign Up, Sign In, and User Profile UIs to their Firebase application.
  - 

  ---

  - [Google Analytics](/guides/development/integrations/analytics/google-analytics)
  - Send user authentication events to your Google Analytics property to enable metrics tracking on user sign up and sign in events.
  - 

  ---

  - [Grafbase](/guides/development/integrations/databases/grafbase)
  - Build applications with serverless GraphQL backends provided by Grafbase and user authentication provided by Clerk.
  - 

  ---

  - [Hasura](/guides/development/integrations/databases/hasura)
  - Easily connect to your Hasura GraphQL endpoint using Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - 

  ---

  - [Inngest](/guides/development/webhooks/inngest)
  - Use events from Clerk to trigger functions defined in your codebase with Inngest. Easily handle data synchronization, onboarding campaigns, or billing workflows in code.
  - 

  ---

  - [Instant](/guides/development/integrations/databases/instantdb)
  - Clerk has a default integration with Instant. You get all the auth features of Clerk, and a relational database you can use directly from the browser.
  - 

  ---

  - [Neon](/guides/development/integrations/databases/neon)
  - Build applications using Neon's serverless Postgres database with Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - 

  ---

  - [Nhost](/guides/development/integrations/databases/nhost)
  - The open source alternative to Firebase with GraphQL seamlessly integrates with Clerk to provide authenticated queries.
  - 

  ---

  - [Prisma Postgres](/guides/development/integrations/databases/prisma-postgres)
  - Build applications using Prisma Postgres with Clerk as your authentication provider.
  - 

  ---

  - [Shopify](/guides/development/integrations/platforms/shopify)
  - Use Clerk as your preferred Auth solution for your Shopify store.
  - 

  ---

  - [Supabase](/guides/development/integrations/databases/supabase)
  - Clerk embraces the open source alternative to Firebase and offers a first-class integration with Supabase.
